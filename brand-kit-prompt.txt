BRAND POSITIONING
Daily Protocol is a single-user personal daily-optimization web app unifying a health/wellness checklist (morning routine, supplements, grooming, deep-work, recovery), meal planning, and personal finance (net worth, budgets, transactions). Audience: disciplined, self-directed individuals who treat health and money as a daily practice. Positioning: a calm, private, almanac-like operating system for one's day — the antithesis of gamified streak apps. Tone: considered, analog, disciplined, warm, editorial. AVOID: gamification badges, neon, purple-blue SaaS gradients, glassmorphism, default rounded cards, dashboard clutter, stock fitness photography.

AESTHETIC CONCEPT
Core art direction: "a printed wellness almanac rendered as a quiet website." Warm cream paper background (#F5F0E8) with near-black ink text (#1A1714), a single load-bearing muted gold accent (#C8A96E), and a deep moss green (#4A5240) for affirmative/secondary states; stone grey (#8A8278) for labels. Typeface pairing: Cormorant Garamond (light airy serif, weight 300, for large editorial headlines and occasional italics) with DM Mono (monospace for body text, UI chrome, numerics, and uppercase wide-letter-spaced section kickers/labels). A barely-there fractal paper-grain texture (~3-4% opacity) over flat color fields. Restraint over richness: impact from scale contrast (huge thin serif vs. tiny tracked mono caps), thin hairline rules (12% ink opacity), and abundant negative space. Memorable idea: hairline-ruled sections with tiny uppercase mono kickers sitting above oversized light-serif statements, like chapter headings in an almanac.
